Before filling out the form for recording a new Biometric device, here is the explaination of some fields for supporting you to fill out with required and correct data about Biometric device

SERIAL NUMBER: These are series of characters used to identify a Biometric device

Tag number:This is a series of characters which identifies each asset of an organization

Entry date in the bank: The date when the bank has received the Biometric device from supplier

Warranty time(in months): The time given by the supplier to the bank for Biometric device to be returned back if it doesn't meet the specifications

Life time (in months): The time the Biometric device is supposed to be used and after that time the computer should not be reliable(provided by the manufacturer and shuould be followed)

Store: If the Biometric device is in stock which stock where it is stored

Purchase price(RWF): Amount that spent while buying Biometric device

Sell price: If the Biometric device was sold, at which price it was sold.

Working status: Behaviour of the Biometric device while being used

Category: The type of Biometric device whether TV or monitor

Biometric device size: The size of the Biometric device in inches
