Before filling out the form for recording a new ATM, here is the explaination of some fields for supporting you to fill out with required and correct data about ATM

SERIAL NUMBER: These are series of characters used to identify a ATM

Tag number:This is a series of characters which identifies each asset of an organization

Entry date in the bank: The date when the bank has received the ATM from supplier

Warranty time(in months): The time given by the supplier to the bank for ATM to be returned back if it doesn't meet the specifications

Life time (in months): The time the ATM is supposed to be used and after that time the computer should not be reliable(provided by the manufacturer and shuould be followed)

Store: If the ATM is in stock which stock where it is stored

Purchase price(RWF): Amount that spent while buying ATM

Sell price: If the ATM was sold, at which price it was sold.

Working status: Behaviour of the ATM while being used

Category: The type of ATM whether TV or monitor

ATM size: The size of the ATM in inches
